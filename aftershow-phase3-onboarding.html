Onboarding. Component Spec
ThemePicker (Onboarding)Molecule
PurposeFirst screen after sign-up. Signals that both themes are equal, first-class choices — not Settings afterthought.
PreviewMini mockup within each option card — shows real typography and color at micro scale
DefaultDark selected on first render — the Marshall amp reference is the brand identity
NamesMarshall Dark · Orange Vintage — amp references baked into the option names themselves
Live previewTapping an option updates the phone shell theme in real time. The whole screen switches.
ContactsImport (Onboarding)Molecule
Copy"Find your people" not "Allow contacts access" — no permission-gate language
Privacy note"Your contacts are checked locally and never uploaded" — inline, not a modal
Result stateShows match count prominently, then "Follow All" or "Choose individually"
SkipAlways skippable. This is onboarding, not a gate.
FirstShowStepOrganism
Gate1 show minimum to unlock Feed — per Phase 2 decision. Low enough to not alienate. High enough to seed value.
From MemoryProminent secondary CTA — the "I want to log old shows" path. No friction, no proof required.
Unlock momentAfter first log: 🔓 animation, personalised copy listing the 3 friends they just found. Feed becomes real.
CTA copy"Go to Feed →" (not "Get Started" or "Finish"). Destination-specific. You know what you're getting.
ProgressDotsAtomic
StatesDone (gold ring) · Active (gold fill) · Next (muted)
Count3 dots for 3 steps. Never more than 3 steps in onboarding.
